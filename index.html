Our Services

Specialized in Electromechanical & General Contractors

Radiography Testing

Radiographic Testing (RT), or industrial radiography, is a nondestructive testing (NDT)
method of inspecting materials for hidden flaws by using Radiation Sources or X Rays.

Heat Treatment

Heat treatment process is specified to remove residual stress from metal parts that result from them being processed. Stress relieving is done on metal components that have been cold forged, machined, welded, etc… before the next operation.

Ultrasonic Testing

Ultrasonic Testing (UT) uses very short ultrasonic pulse-waves ranging from 0.1-15 MHz and occasionally up to 50 MHz are transmitted into materials to detect internal flaws or to characterize materials

Magnetic Particle Testing

Magnetic particle Inspection (MPI) is a non-destructive testing (NDT) process for detecting surface and slightly subsurface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials such as iron, nickel, cobalt, and some of their alloys.

Dye Penetrant Testing

Dye penetrant inspection (DPI), also called liquid penetrant inspection (LPI) or penetrant testing (PT), is a widely applied and low-cost inspection method used to locate surface-breaking defects in all non-porous materials (metals, plastics, or ceramics).
